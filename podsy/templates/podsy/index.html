About

We're a new community.

We created Podsy to serve the world of podcasts in two fundamental areas:

  • Pre-Podsy, the web didn't have a central, go-to place for podcasts, like YouTube is for videos or like Reddit is for cats. A number of sites have tried, and so has iTunes, but none have reached the potential of what we think can be done.
  • We're here to provide not just a repository of audio content, but a community that is dynamic, interactive, and helpful to listeners' lives.
Not only do we strive to be a reliable resource for educational audio content, we also make it very easy for knowledge seekers to connect with those who already possess that knowledge. To facilitate that, we've streamlined the process for finding content, for following the users/categories/tags you're interested in, and for creating new content.
